# SuperStore-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard (Interactive Dashboard creation using Microsoft Excel)
## Project Objective
This project aims to evaluate Superstore sales performance and profitability by region, segment, and product category, providing insights that support data-driven business decisions.

## Dataset used
- <a href="https://github.com/Timi-23/SuperStore-Sales-Analysis-Dashbord/blob/11da1092b17a6bb2d429195dd3789b1ca4ae3324/SuperStore%20Cleaned%20Data.xlsx">Dataset</a>

##Question (KPIs)
- What is the total sales, profit, and quantity generated over the period?
- How has profit changed year over year and month over month?
- Which year recorded the highest and lowest profit?
- Which regions and states generate the highest sales?
- What are the top 10 states by total sales?
- Which customer segment contributes the most to sales and profit?
- How does each customer segment perform in terms of quantity sold?
- Which product categories generate the most sales and profit?
- Are there product categories with high sales but low profit?
- What is the overall profit margin of the business?
- How many transactions included discounts?
- How does discount usage potentially impact profitability?
- Which time periods show profit growth or decline?
- How balanced is performance across regions, segments, and categories?
